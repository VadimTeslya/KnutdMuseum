GUIDED TOURS

GUIDED TOURS

General exhibition tours are conducted by qualified art centre guides, who offer comprehensive information about all works presented in the museum.

Tuesday - Friday at 19:00
Saturday - Sunday at 14:00, 17:00 and 19:00

THEME TOURS

Theme tours is a special format of the museum tours, during which visitors focus on and analyze several selected works. This approach helps viewers to better understand objects and to explore photographer’s individual style and practice.

All tours are available free of charge. For further information and schedule please check our webpage.

TOURS FOR SCHOOLS AND UNIVERSITIES

For schools and universities we offer special tours tailored according to the age and educational needs of the participants. To inscribe a group leader needs to fill in the form available at the reception. Maximum number of children in the group is 25.

Tuesday - Friday at 13:00

Please reserve in advance by mailing info@KNUTD.org or phoning +38 (044) 590 08 58. Tours are available in English, Ukrainian and Russian.
