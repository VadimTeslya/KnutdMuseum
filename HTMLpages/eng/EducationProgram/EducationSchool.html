SCHOOL AND UNIVERSITIES

UNDERSTANDING THE MUSEUM – TEACHER’S EDITION

Within the exhibition KNUTD museum suggests a specially developed summer programme for teachers aimed to research the museum as an alternative learning environment, stimulate creative and critical responses, offering cross-curricular connections and specific subject focus.

KNUTD PHOTO MUSEUM’S TEACHER’S TUESDAYS

KNUTD museum suggests a set of practical and theoretical activities providing an exploration of physical, intellectual and emotional aspects of contemporary photos shown at KNUTD photo museum. Bringing the education team and teachers together, KNUTD photo museum provides a bridge between the classroom and the photo gallery on a regular basis in order to identify strategies for engaging children in photo.

Teacher’s Tuesdays are held monthly on Tuesdays at 14:00.

KNUTD PHOTO MUSEUM GOES ACADEMY

Within the present exhibition, KNUTD photo museum continues to develop its dialogue with students. In such a format artists under training get an opportunity to make an photographers statement while the curators get a chance to meet the youngest generation.
