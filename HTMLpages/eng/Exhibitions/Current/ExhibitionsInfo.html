PHOTOGRAPHY THROUGH THE AGES

DATE:

TIME:

LOCATION:

Thursday, April 7, 2016

8 p.m.

Dryden Theatre

On Sunday, April 17, at 15:00 the KNUTD invites children 3-11 years old with special needs (social and intellectual development disorders: mental and speech retardation, mental retardation, Down syndrome, autism) to engage ArtFriends educational program on "House, in where I live. " During the meeting participants on the example of classical landscapes will review the history of changes in the exterior and interior, will talk about their own living space. In the second part of the session the children will take part in the quest and play outdoor games. The creative part of our guests create the installation "My house."

The event will begin at 15:00 on the 5th floor in the "Educational room." Occupation will guide art center Olga Shishlova.

Participation is free. The maximum number of children in the group of 8 participants.

Registration is required. For the record and for more information, please contact the following contacts: Tel .: +38 044 590-08-50 E-mail: info@pknutd.org

The purpose ArtFriends classes - to help the participants join the cultural flow on a par with the other participants; develop and improve communication skills; harmonize the psycho-emotional sphere through interaction with art objects; in an unobtrusive way to introduce the world of contemporary art.
